Operational Case Studies

The problem, constraints, solution, and outcome.

Each project began with a real operational gap and was shaped around the people, data, rules, and decisions involved.

Mission PMIS

Turning scattered project information into owner-side intelligence

Problem
Building assessments, risks, shutdowns, questions, and readiness information were distributed across separate records.
Constraints
Healthcare operations, multiple stakeholders, phased work, and the need to preserve Excel as the source of truth.
Solution
A connected Excel backend and application-style front end with campus, building, reporting, and executive views.
Outcome
A single operational framework for field review, leadership visibility, and project readiness across 35+ buildings.

City InspectorPro

Consolidating municipal inspection and enforcement operations

Problem
Permits, inspections, complaints, public guidance, SOPs, and code references were fragmented.
Constraints
Limited staff capacity, public accountability, varied workflows, and the need for accessible records.
Solution
A full-stack municipal portal combining internal operations, GIS, public resources, dashboards, and AI support.
Outcome
A unified model for managing municipal fieldwork, institutional knowledge, and public-facing service delivery.

QA Management Manual

Converting federal specifications into field-operational guidance

Problem
Critical quality requirements existed across lengthy specifications without a single owner-side operating reference.
Constraints
Federal healthcare standards, project-specific obligations, inspection rigor, and traceable documentation.
Solution
A 451-page technical publication with 40 operational sections, standardized procedures, and 12 appendices.
Outcome
A reusable framework for teaching the why, defining the how, and supporting consistent owner QA/QC execution.
