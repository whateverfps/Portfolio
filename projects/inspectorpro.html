MUNICIPAL TECHNOLOGY

Hanover InspectorPro

A browser-based concept for organizing municipal rental housing inspection operations around the ordinance and the work.

Open live system ↗
Inspection OperationsMunicipal WorkflowProduct Prototype
ROLE
Product Designer · Domain Analyst · Builder
PROBLEM
Municipal inspection work depends on records, timing, enforcement, and communication that are often fragmented.
OUTCOME
A tangible product concept connecting properties, inspections, complaints, violations, and certificates.
TOOLS
HTML · CSS · JavaScript · AI-assisted development

THE CHALLENGE

Translate an ordinance into an operating system.

The objective was to move beyond a basic inspection form and imagine how a municipal department could manage the full lifecycle of rental housing oversight in a more structured, visible, and user-friendly way.

THE APPROACH

Design from the inspector's real decisions.

The prototype organized the work around the information and actions an inspector must manage every day.

  • Structured property and ownership records.
  • Connected inspections, complaints, violations, and certificates.
  • Designed dashboard views for workload and status.
  • Used ordinance-driven workflow concepts.
  • Focused on clarity for inspectors, administrators, and leadership.

THE RESULT

A credible digital transformation concept.

InspectorPro shows how domain expertise can be converted into a product vision quickly, with enough structure and polish to make the future workflow visible to decision-makers.

WHAT THIS DEMONSTRATES

Product thinking grounded in operations.

  • Domain analysis
  • User-centered workflow design
  • Rapid prototyping
  • Information architecture
  • Interface design
  • Stakeholder communication
